# 1. Introduction

FinVest AI is an AI-powered mutual fund investment platform that helps users invest with confidence through intelligent recommendations, live market insights, portfolio analytics, goal planning, and SIP management.

Unlike traditional investment platforms, FinVest AI focuses on education, transparency, and explainable AI to guide investors toward better financial decisions.

The platform is designed around one philosophy:

> Users should never feel lost.

Every screen must answer one or more of the following:

• What is happening in the market?

• How is my portfolio performing?

• What should I do next?

• Why is the AI recommending this?

---

# 2. Product Vision

Our mission is to help users invest with confidence.

We aim to build an intelligent investment companion rather than a traditional investment dashboard.

Every feature must provide one or more of the following:

• Knowledge

• Insight

• Action

• Confidence

---

# 3. Actors

The following actors interact with the platform.

Guest

A visitor exploring FinVest AI without creating an account.

Investor

A registered user who manages investments and portfolios.

Administrator

Manages the entire platform.

FinVest AI Advisor

Provides intelligent recommendations and explanations.

Market Data Service

Supplies real-time market and mutual fund data.

Notification Service

Handles email and platform notifications.
